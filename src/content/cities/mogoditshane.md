Mogoditshane is a rapidly growing settlement immediately west of [Gaborone](/gaborone), Botswana's capital, and functionally part of the capital's suburban expansion. It runs on Central Africa Time, UTC+2, year-round with no daylight saving, the same as all of [Botswana](/country/botswana).

## suburban expansion west of Gaborone

The settlement has grown quickly as Gaborone's land prices and density have pushed housing development outward. Mogoditshane is now one of Botswana's most populous urban areas, with residents commuting daily into the capital. The commute follows a clock-based schedule: the working day in Gaborone sets the rhythm for suburban Mogoditshane.

Botswana's economic success, driven by diamond mining managed through the De Beers partnership, has created one of Africa's strongest middle classes. That prosperity shows in the suburban growth patterns of places like Mogoditshane: new housing estates, shopping centers, and the infrastructure of a stable, growing economy.

[Maun](/maun) to the northwest shares this timezone. [9am in Mogoditshane is 7am in London](/when/9am-mogoditshane/london) during European winter.
