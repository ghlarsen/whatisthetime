Bimbo is a city directly southwest of [Bangui](/bangui), the capital of the Central African Republic, separated from it by little more than administrative lines. The two cities function as a single urban agglomeration. Both run on West Africa Time: UTC+1, year-round, no daylight saving adjustments.

The Central African Republic sits almost exactly on the 4th parallel north, barely above the equator. At this latitude, daylight varies very little through the year. Sunrise in Bimbo falls between 5:30am and 6am regardless of season. Sunset arrives between 5:30pm and 6:30pm. The sun rises and sets quickly here, with minimal twilight, because the sun crosses the horizon at a steep angle. UTC+1 keeps solar noon close to 1pm, which is a reasonable alignment for this meridian.

## The Ubangi River and the Congo border

Bimbo sits near the Ubangi River, which forms the border between the Central African Republic and the Democratic Republic of Congo. Brazzaville and Kinshasa are visible across the water. The DRC uses UTC+1 in its western regions, so the timezone boundary here is not a national border but lies further east, within the DRC itself.

The region has been affected by the prolonged instability that has plagued the Central African Republic since 2012. Despite this, Bimbo's suburban markets, its river traffic, and its connection to Bangui's administrative life continue to function on their own calendar.

## A near-equatorial clock

For people living this close to the equator, clock time and solar time align well throughout the year. There is no midsummer midnight sun, no winter afternoon darkness. The seasons are wet and dry, not light and dark. The clock matters in a different way here: it structures the workday and the market schedule, but it doesn't define when light is available the way it does at higher latitudes.

The [time difference between Bimbo and London](/when/9am-bimbo/london) is one hour in winter, zero in summer when the UK observes BST. [Carnot](/carnot) is further west in the same country on the same clock.
